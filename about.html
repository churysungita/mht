    About us

    ABOUT US

    Mental Health Tanzania

    Mental Health Tanzania has two main Focuses/Drives/motives which are Mental Health and Mental Illnesses. Based on those two drives, MHT focuses on the following priorities:

    Mental Health
  • Advocating on Mental Health
  • Promoting Mental Health
  • Supporting on Mental Health issues &victims

    • Mental Illnesses
  • Intervention of Mental Illnesses
  • Prevention measures on Mental Illnesses
  • Management of Mental Illnesses
  • Assessments of Mental Illnesses and psychological challenges
  • Diagnosis of Mental Illnesses
  • Treatment of Mental Illnesses

    • Our Mission

    To serve, support, advocate and promote the wellbeing and mental health through prevention measures, interventions, management, diagnosis and treatment of mental health related problems and psychological challenges.

    Our Vision

    To see the community where everyone gets access to quality and reliable mental health services and psychosocial support.
