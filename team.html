    Winfrida Erasto

    Mental Health Advocacy Country Coordinator(MHT)

    Winfrida is a mental health activist. In 2017 she was diagnosed with depression; it changed her life in a way that made her want to learn more about mental health. She does volunteer work with different people and organizations in bringing mental health awareness to the society. She loves traveling and reading books, she’s passionate about mental health activism because it helps her talk, listen, and connect to people in a deeper level. She joined MHT team since 2019, she has organized and completed different projects and events in the organization in different times. Professionally she is a labour officer , with a degree in Labour Relation. She wants to reach every single person she can get and make them aware of mental health, that's where she sees herself in the future.

    Desdery K. Peter

    Regional Mental Health Clubs Coordinator in Dar Es Salaam(MHT)

    Desidery has worked with MHT as Reginal Mental Health Clubs Coordinator in Dar Es Salaam since 2021 and he has organized different mental health clubs and projects among youth. Desdery holds a Diploma in Counselling Psychology, he has experience working with different organizations such Community Concern Training Institute (CCTI) in Nairobi Kenya, focusing on HIV/AIDs and TB. He has also worked with PASADA Organization as a HIV/AIDS Counsellor and status tester in St Benedict Hospital Dar es salaam. He loves working with children and youths and interrogate them friendly which helps him to find out what they go through and find solutions for them.
